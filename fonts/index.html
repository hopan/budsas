Unicode Fonts for Romanized Viet-Pali-Sanskrit

* VU-Times (serif), 4 typestyles (normal, italic, bold, bold-italic), clear and crisp, suitable for screen display and in printed documents, similar to Times New Roman, by Ho Phuoc Hung, USA (http://www.vn.net ).

Download: vu-times-vp (658 Kb)

* VU-Arial (sans-serif), 4 typestyles (normal, italic, bold, bold-italic), clear and crisp, suitable for screen display and in printed documents, similar to Arial, by Ho Phuoc Hung, USA (http://www.vn.net ).

Download: vu-arial-vp (636 Kb)

* CN-Times (serif) and CN-Arial (sans-serif), 4 typestyles, by Chan-Nguyen, Germany (http://www.daouyen.com ).

* Sanvijjo (serif) and Doilagiang (sans-serif), 4 typestyles, by Bhikkhu Minh-Tinh, Vietnam.

Download: mt-sanvijjo-vp.zip (427 Kb)

* Carslon Roman (serif), normal, bold, italic, by George Williams (http://bibliofile.duhs.duke.edu/gww/fonts/fonts.html ).

* Code2000 (serif), normal typestyle only, by James Kass (http://home.att.net/~jameskass/ ).

* Element (sans-serif), normal & bold typestyles, by Bhikkhu Pesala (http://www.aimwell.org).

* Garland (serif), 4 typestyles (normal, italic, bold, bold-italic), by Bhikkhu Pesala (http://www.aimwell.org).

* Gentium (serif), normal, bold, italic, by Victor Gaultney (http://www.sil.org/~gaultney/gentium/ ).

* SImPL (fixed width, Courier-like), normal typestyle only, by Phil Chasteney (http://www.vector.org.uk/resource/simp2.htm ).

* TITUS Cyberbit (serif), normal typestyle only, by Bitstream (ftp://ftp.netscape.com/pub/communicator/extras/fonts/windows/ ).

* Thryomanes (serif), 4 typestyles, by Herman Miller (http://www.io.com/~hmiller/lang/ ).

* Tahoma (sans-serif), only nomal and bold typestyles, by Microsoft, included in recent versions of Windows (Win Me, 2000, XP, ...).

* Arial Unicode MS (sans-serif), only normal typestyle, by Microsoft. This font is included with recent versions of Microsoft Office suite (Office 2000 or later). Arial Unicode MS is a full Unicode font, containing approximately 40,000 alphabetical characters, ideographic characters (CJK, Chinese-Japanese-Korean), and symbols as defined in the Unicode 2.1 standard. Total size: 13,687 Kb.

* To check for the availability of Viet-Pali-Sanskrit characters of each font, download: viet-phan_spec.zip
